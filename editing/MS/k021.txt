Central claim unit: a5
Original order:
e1 pro 'Property entails commitment' is one of the tenets of our social order.
e2 opp Everyone should be able to get the utmost profit from his or her property.
e3 pro A fixing of rent prices according to the market alone, however, will lead to a further division of society.
e4 pro The rich and poor live in different neighbourhoods.
e5 pro A constant increase in rent upon a change of tenants hence cannot be desired by society.
