Central claim unit: a1
Original order:
e1 pro Supermarkets and shopping centres should be allowed to open on any Sundays and holidays.
e2 opp This may be a change for the employees that cannot to be implemented easily.
e3 pro However, especially for working customers this would mean greater flexibility and fewer nerve-racking peak times during the week.
e4 pro Considering the growing digitization of society, the traditional model of the weekend will soon be obsolete.
e5 pro Many European countries, for instance the Netherlands, Sweden and Great Britain, have already understood this and successfully adjusted the opening hours for retailers.
